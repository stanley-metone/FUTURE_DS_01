# 📊 FUTURE_DS_01 – Metrix Sales Performance Insights

## 📌 Project Overview

FUTURE_DS_01 is the first task completed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship program.

The objective of this task was to analyze real-world business sales data (Superstore dataset) and transform raw data into meaningful, actionable insights using Power BI.

This project focuses on solving practical business problems rather than just creating visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Addressed

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- How do sales and profit change over time?
- Which categories and regions are most profitable?
- How do discounts impact profitability?
- Where should the business focus to drive faster growth?

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

- Cleaned and organized raw sales data
- Ensured data consistency and formatting
- Created calculated measures using DAX
- Built dynamic KPI indicators

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Executive KPI Overview (Revenue, Profit, Margin %, Orders, Avg Discount)
- Monthly Revenue & Profit Trend Analysis
- Top-Selling Products with Ranking & Margin %
- Sales & Profit Mix by Category
- Regional Revenue Contribution Analysis
- Profitability Health Indicator (Gauge)
- Interactive Slicers
- Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Technology category delivers the strongest profit margins.
- West region contributes the highest revenue share.
- High discount levels negatively impact overall profitability.
- Focusing on top-performing products can accelerate business growth.
